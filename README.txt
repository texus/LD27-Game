In this game you have to race from checkpoint to checkpoint within 10 seconds.
There are currently 3 levels and you can choose between 3 different cars.

Use the up arrow to accelerate and the left/right arrows to steer.


Libraries used: SFML + Thor + TGUI


Music and car images were downloaded from internet.
Music: http://www.nosoapradio.us/
